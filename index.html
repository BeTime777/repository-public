We collect the following data:

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information through our partners:

  • Application usage data (Firebase Analytics)

We use your data to:

Important: We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services Used

Our application uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: authentication management
  • Firebase Database: game data storage
  • Firebase Analytics: usage analysis
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 AdMob and Advertising Mediation

  • Google AdMob: ad display
  • Mediation Partners: third-party ad networks
  • These services may collect advertising identifiers and usage data
  • AdMob Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to personalize ads. You can manage your advertising preferences in your iOS device settings.

3.3 Advertising Identifier (IDFA)

The WinTime application uses the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) provided by Apple to measure advertising campaign performance and display more relevant ads. This identifier may be collected by Google AdMob and partner ad networks through the mediation system.

Before any collection or use of this identifier, the application displays Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent popup. No advertising data is shared until the user has given explicit consent.

If the user chooses to decline tracking, the ads displayed will be non-personalized and no advertising identifier will be transmitted.

You can change your choice at any time in your iOS device privacy settings: Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely via Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform) located in the European Union when possible.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction.

5. Retention Period

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service.

After deleting your account, your personal data is deleted within a maximum of 30 days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: correct your inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: delete your data
  • Right to restriction: limit the processing of your data
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data
  • Right to data portability: retrieve your data in a structured format

You can also withdraw your consent to advertising tracking at any time through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
